Rules for Conway's Game of Life:

  • Live cells with two or three neighbors continue to live.
  • Empty cells with three neighbors come to life.

Click on a cell to toggle it between live and dead.
The random button will fill the grid with 20% live cells.
The wrap checkbox determines whether cells at the edge have neighbors on the other side of the grid.
The auto checkbox will automatically do a random fill when the cells are stable or just flipping between two states.
